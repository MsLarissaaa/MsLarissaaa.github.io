Welcome!

Thanks for visiting my site! My name is Larissa and I'm currently studying at Dev Bootcamp to become a web developer. I'm a proud member of the Chicago Grasshoppers cohort of DBC. My goal is to become a full-stack developer with proficiency in Ruby, JavaScript and SQL languages by the time I graduate. I'm interested in virtually all aspects of programming and web design, and so far I love what I'm learning!

Prior to starting DBC, I graduated with honors from the University of Chicago, concentrating in History and Latin American Studies. I also worked for two years as a paralegal in the Immigration law practice of Sidley Austin LLP. In addition to coding, my hobbies include reading, teaching yoga, and spending time with my dog. Thanks again for stopping by and please check out my About Me page for more info!
